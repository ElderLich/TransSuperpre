[name]
S-Force Sorted World

[desc]
If you would banish a card(s) from the hand to activate the effect of an "S-Force" monster you control, you can send 1 "S-Force" card from your Deck to the GY instead, except "S-Force Sorted World". If another card(s) is banished (except during the Damage Step): You can target 1 card in your opponent's field or GY; banish it, then you can move 1 "S-Force" monster you control to another of your Main Monster Zones. You can only use each effect of "S-Force Sorted World" once per turn.
