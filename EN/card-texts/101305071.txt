[name]
Power Patron Shadows of the End Times

[desc]
Special Summon 1 "Power Patron" monster from your hand, face-up Extra Deck, or GY, then you can apply these effects, in sequence, based on the number of different card types (Fusion, Synchro, Xyz) you control.
● 1+: Banish (face-down) the top 3 cards of your opponent's Deck.
● 2+: Banish (face-down) 1 card your opponent controls.
● 3: Banish (face-down) 1 random card from your opponent's hand.
You can only activate 1 "Power Patron Shadows of the End Times" per turn.
