[name]
Capsule Monster Chess

[desc]
During the Main Phase: The turn player can target 1 monster in their GY; place it face-up in their Spell & Trap Zone as a Continuous Spell. During the End Phase: The turn player can send 1 face-up Monster Card they control to the GY; that player Special Summons 1 monster from their Deck with the same original Type and Attribute, but 1, 2, or 3 original Levels higher. You can only use each effect of "Capsule Monster Chess" once per turn.
